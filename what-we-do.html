What We do

We’re more than just a boutique event-planning firm. We’re a global team with the creative capacity to design and execute an unforgettable party — whether a destination wedding, a corporate gig or a big-bash birthday. We believe in creating unique moments that inspire. What makes us different? We listen to you. We then design and create distinctive events based on your specific needs and desires. We transform environments with our mix of solutions that integrate lighting, furnishings, linens and floral design. And we work with some of the best designers, fabricators and chefs in the business to bring experiences to life. We see our partners as more than just collaborators — they’re our friends. As are you.
PARTIES & EVENTS Planning a social or corporate event can be stressful if you have to be both the host and the planner. The key to a successful event is to have someone there to handle all of the production aspects so that you can enjoy your guests or be a host to your VIPs. We’re able to plan any type of event, whether it’s an anniversary, birthday, reunion, sales incentive, holiday celebration or just an old-fashioned party.
WEDDINGS Weddings can be celebrated anywhere from the backyard of your childhood summer home to an exotic locale somewhere deep in the jungle or overlooking the ocean. Whether you’re planning a destination wedding or a wedding close to home, chances are you’ll probably want to extend the celebration over a few days so that you can spend one-on-one time with each of your guests. At Alison Events, we have the resources to assist you in finding the perfect location for your wedding, whether it’s a white sand beach in Mexico, a rustic barn in the Napa Valley, a private villa in the hills of Tuscany, a boutique hotel in London, or your favorite ski lodge in Colorado. If you’ve already found your ideal location, we’ll help you with finding the most talented vendors to fit your personality, budget and aesthetic.
